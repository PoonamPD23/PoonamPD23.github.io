About Me

My research integrates a passion for biological complexities with a focus on Drosophila genetics and stem cell biology. I strive to uncover fundamental mechanisms in developmental biology and contribute to scientific understanding in this dynamic field.

Researcher in Developmental, Metabolism and Stem Cell Biology

I am interested in probing how cells interact with and signal each other in diverse contexts: I'm particularly inclined towards understanding cellular interactions, with implications for regenerative medicine and progression of disease.

My graduate training at the Indian Institute of Science Education and Research (IISER), Mohali has been a blend of rigorous academic training and interdisciplinary research. I specialize in applying experimental methods such as immunohistochemistry and molecular biology techniques to understand biological systems, with a focus on model organisms like D. melanogaster and E. coli .

I am passionate about contributing to the scientific community through collaborative projects and educational initiatives. My research aims to provide insights into developmental processes and their implications in understanding life at a molecular level.
