Fieldwork in Science Education

Jan 2023 - April 2023

This comprehensive research project was conducted to understand the unique challenges faced by science communicators in India. The study involved in-depth Zoom interviews with five diverse professionals in the field of science communication, including a Science Illustrator, a Science Editor & Journalist, a Freelance Science Writer, a Scientific Filmmaker, and a Science Author who also works as a Communication Consultant. The primary goal was to delve into the experiences of these communicators and uncover the specific hurdles they encounter in their work.
